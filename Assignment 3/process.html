Process

My process of designing revolves around creative problem solving. I like to solve problems with design that is aesthetically pleasing and that makes sense. I like to see what the needs of the client are and see how I can create a solution that suits their needs. I like to do creative sketches in the beginning phases, and I like to create moodboards as I go deeper into the process. In addition, I create comps of different ideas that will allow me to gather client feedback to get a direction on which way to go with a project.
