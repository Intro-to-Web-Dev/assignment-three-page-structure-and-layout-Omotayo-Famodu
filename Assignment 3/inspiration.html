Process

My inspiration for artwork ranges from other designers to nature. I am very inspired by abstract expressionists like Willem De Kooning, Francis Bacon, Basquiat, and Eddie Martinez. I love how different artists have techniques and skills of utilizing the elements and principles of design to create their works. Im inspired by nature because it allows me to see patterns that exist in real life and to see colors and patterns form naturally.
